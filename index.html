About Us

Empowering Childcare Success for Nearly Four Decades

With over 38 years of experience in the childcare industry, ACP Shared Services has been a pillar of support for Group Family Day Care providers in Brooklyn and Queens. As a sister company of A Child's Place Day Care Center, we have trained and supported numerous educators who have gone on to establish their own successful programs.
